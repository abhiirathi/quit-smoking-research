# Premium Dopamine Features — What People Will Pay For

The free CRAVE button gets users hooked on the substitute. But for retention + revenue, we need **paid features that deliver bigger dopamine hits than a cigarette and bigger value than ₹999 lifetime alone**. These also build a moat — none of the 87 competitor apps offer any of the below.

Each feature is rated:
- 🧠 **Dopamine value** — how strong the neurochemical reward is
- 💰 **Willingness to pay** — Indian-market evidence (existing categories where users already pay)
- 🛡️ **Moat** — how hard to copy

---

## 1. Skill-money tournaments — "Focus Arena" 🥇

**The pitch:** 60-second mental-skill mini-games (memory, reaction, pattern, focus) with **real cash prizes**. Daily/weekly leaderboards. Entry fee ₹10–₹50; prize pool ₹500–₹50,000.

**Why it works:**
- Smokers' #1 self-justification is "I smoke to focus." We turn that into a **provable, paid contest** — they prove their focus *without* smoking, and earn money doing it.
- This is the same dopamine architecture as Dream11, MPL, WinZO — categories Indians already spend ₹40,000+ Cr/year on.
- **Critical:** skill-based, not chance-based → legal in India under the Public Gambling Act exemption (Supreme Court 2021).
- Each tournament entry = a 60-sec dopamine hit + variable monetary reward + ego competition. Triple stack.

**Revenue model:** Take 15–25% rake on entry fees + tournament sponsorship.

**Defensibility:** The dataset of *which games predict quit success* is irreplicable. After 6 months of data we know which skill drills correlate with 90-day abstinence — that becomes our prescription engine.

🧠 9/10 · 💰 10/10 · 🛡️ 8/10

## 2. The Stack — branded dopamine-repair subscription 🧪

**The pitch:** Monthly box of nutraceuticals delivered to your door. Magnesium Glycinate + L-Theanine + L-Tyrosine + Rhodiola, dosed for the dopamine-recovery protocol from your interview notes. ₹799/mo or ₹6,999/year.

**Why it works:**
- The interview gave us the science — we operationalize it.
- Recurring revenue, high margin (white-label cost ~₹150/box, sells for ₹799).
- **Affiliate vs. private label**: Amazon affiliate = 4–8%. Our own brand = 60–70% gross margin.
- DTC supplement market in India growing 30% YoY; HealthKart, MuscleBlaze, Fast&Up are proof of consumer trust.
- Each delivery = anticipation dopamine. We track adherence in-app and tie it to streak rewards.

**Revenue model:** ₹799/month subscription, ~70% margin = ~₹560 contribution per user per month.

**Defensibility:** We're the only quit-app that's also the supplement brand. The supplement is *part of the product*, not an afterthought.

🧠 6/10 · 💰 9/10 · 🛡️ 7/10

## 3. Skin-in-the-game commitment bet — "Quit Pact" 🎯

**The pitch:** User puts ₹500–₹5,000 on the line. If they stay smoke-free for 30 days, they get it all back **+ a 20% bonus from the pool**. If they relapse, the money goes to (a) a charity they actively dislike, (b) the prize pool for other users who succeed, or (c) us.

**Why it works:**
- **Loss aversion** is 2x stronger than gain motivation (Kahneman). This is the most-studied behavior-change mechanic in academia.
- Validated model: stickK (Yale economists), HealthyWage. Indian apps haven't deployed this for smoking specifically.
- The "give my money to a charity I hate" framing (anti-charity) doubles success rates in published studies.
- Each successful day = anticipation dopamine ("I'm winning my money back"). Each near-miss = visceral relief = dopamine spike.

**Revenue model:** 10% platform fee on each pact + spread on the redistribution.

**Defensibility:** Trust + escrow infrastructure. Once users have ₹5,000 with us, switching cost is real.

🧠 8/10 · 💰 8/10 · 🛡️ 6/10

## 4. Voice-cloned AI coach — "Speak in their voice" 🎙️

**The pitch:** User uploads a 60-second voice sample of someone who matters — their child, spouse, parent, late father. Our AI clones that voice and delivers personalized anti-smoking nudges in *that* voice. *"Papa, please don't. You promised."* spoken in their actual 8-year-old's actual voice. ₹499 one-time + ₹99/mo for unlimited messages.

**Why it works:**

- Voice cloning tech is now ₹0.01/sec — accessible.
- Emotionally devastating in the right way. Once the user has set this up, the switching cost is psychological, not financial.
- No competitor in the world is doing this for quit-smoking.

**Revenue model:** ₹499 setup + ₹99/mo for daily fresh messages. Conversion will be high among parents.

**Defensibility:** The voice clone is permanently associated with our app. They will not redo the upload elsewhere.

⚠️ **Ethics:** consent mandatory. We require the loved one to record the sample directly + opt in via SMS confirmation. No surreptitious cloning.

## 5. NRT-as-a-service — done-for-you taper 💊

**The pitch:** Nicotine gum / lozenge subscription (Nicorette, Nicotex) with a personalized tapering schedule shipped monthly. Doctor consultation included for ₹99 first visit, prescription handled. ₹1,499/month.

**Why it works:**
- Removes the 3 actual barriers to NRT use: (1) figuring out the dose, (2) buying it (embarrassment + access), (3) tapering correctly. We solve all three.
- DTC pharma works in India: Mfine, PharmEasy, Truemeds. Quit-smoking is a *narrow, high-intent* DTC niche with no dominant player.
- Nicorette gum 4mg ×105 strips = ₹1,200 retail; we bulk-buy at ~₹600. Margin is real.
- This is the only paid feature that addresses the **chemical** while everything else addresses dopamine.

**Revenue model:** ₹1,499/month subscription, ~₹500–700 margin per user.

**Defensibility:** Pharma supply chain + telehealth license is a regulatory moat.

## 6. Craving wearable — partner with a smart band 📿

**The pitch:** Partner with a cheap fitness tracker maker (Boat, Noise, Fire-Boltt) for a co-branded band. The band detects **HRV spikes that precede a craving by 5–10 minutes** and triggers our app's CRAVE flow *before* the user consciously feels the urge. ₹2,999 one-time + ₹99/mo data subscription.

**Why it works:**
- Pre-empting the craving is dramatically more effective than reacting to it (peer-reviewed: "anticipatory intervention").
- Indian wearable market is one of the cheapest in the world; partnership brings hardware cost to ₹600 unit.
- Hardware in the user's hand = daily product touchpoint = retention.
- Insurance partnerships unlock when there's a wearable: HRV data → premium discounts (Aditya Birla, ICICI Lombard already do this).

**Revenue model:** ₹2,999 hardware (₹800 margin) + ₹99/mo data subscription + future insurance affiliate revenue.

**Defensibility:** Hardware moat — a software-only competitor can't replicate.

# Top 3 to build first (after the free CRAVE button)

If we have to pick three premium features for v1.5–v2 (next 6 months):

### 🥇 Focus Arena (skill-money tournaments)
Highest dopamine, highest WTP, fits Indian gaming culture, defensible data moat. Build first.

### 🥈 The Stack (branded supplements subscription)
Highest margin, highest LTV, the supplement angle is the only one that addresses *baseline dopamine repair*. Build the brand alongside the app.

### 🥉 Voice-cloned coach
Most emotionally sticky feature. Lowest tech risk (off-the-shelf voice cloning APIs). High differentiation.

These three alone, at modest conversion (5% of MAU on Stack, 20% of MAU playing one tournament/week, 2% buying voice coach), generate ₹150–₹250 ARPU/mo — putting us at premium-app revenue with a fundamentally healthier user.

# What we're NOT building (and why)

- **Subscription-only model** — competitors' #1 source of bad reviews. We use one-time + à la carte premium.
- **NFT / token rewards** — dead, untrusted in India.
- **Generic content library** — every app has it, no one reads it in a craving.
- **Social feed** — moderation hell, low-value, dilutes the dopamine focus.
